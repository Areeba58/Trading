Frequently Asked Questions
Explore our comprehensive FAQ to find answers to common queries.
What is the best time to trade in the stock market?
The best time to trade depends on the market and strategy, but many traders prefer the first and last hour of the trading session for high volatility and liquidity.
How can I minimize risks while trading?
To minimize risks, use stop-loss orders, diversify your investments, stay updated with market news, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.
What are the best indicators for technical analysis?
Popular indicators include Moving Averages, Relative Strength Index (RSI), MACD, Bollinger Bands, and Fibonacci Retracements for identifying trends and entry points.
